ABOUT

Welcome, I’m Edna Shindinde, personal style advisor & wardrobe editor. I help women embrace their value by teaching how to build a staple wardrobe in line with their inner power and beauty. The greatest flair of mine is to see talent and strength behind every woman I meet. Whether you build a career, take care of a family, own a business or enjoy life by your rules – you are a high-value woman. You set a precedent & should own it proudly! My vision is for each women to unblushingly celebrate her beauty, to be seen and respected without doubting own looks. Styling is just one way to achieve that. And I couldn’t ask for a better mission, as to guide you on this journey.

  • Nadiya Maltseva

    Maltseva Consulting

    During a stage in my life, I needed to transition from my corporate attire during work hours and floral dresses during leisure time, to a more versatile and easy-to-wear style. Edna surprised me by first wanting to understand me as a person, not just my daily role. As we sorted through my closet, I was apprehensive about having to let go of my favorite pieces. However, Edna approached each item with reverence and asked about my personal connection and association with it. In the end, we ended up donating 70% of the items and did a little shopping. Edna goes beyond just putting together an outfit. She taught me to view each item as an ingredient that can be used in various combinations, making them incredibly satisfying and appealing, resulting in compliments from others
